## Research Interests

My research focuses on **Natural Language Processing** and **Machine Learning**. I am particularly interested in the following areas:

### LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
I investigate how to enhance the reasoning capabilities of large language models through reinforcement learning techniques. My work on SynLogic demonstrates how to synthesize verifiable reasoning data at scale for learning logical reasoning.

### Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)
I study the hallucination phenomenon in vision-language models, particularly in chart understanding tasks. My research identifies perception bottlenecks in VLMs and proposes solutions to improve their reliability.

### LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability
I explore the internal mechanisms of large language models related to truthfulness. My work on the Universal Truthfulness Hyperplane reveals fundamental structures inside LLMs that distinguish truthful from untruthful outputs.

### Graph Neural Networks
*(New Research Direction)*

I am expanding my research into Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), exploring how graph-structured data and neural network approaches can be combined with NLP techniques for enhanced representation learning and reasoning.
